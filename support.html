Frequently Asked Questions

How does card matching work?
Swipe through your randomly generated workout cards to match with exercises you want to complete and skip the ones you don't. The app saves this data to tailor your workout summaries.

Where is my workout data saved?
Your preferences and matched exercises are safely stored locally on your device to build your preference summary history.
