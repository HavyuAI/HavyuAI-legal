Frequently Asked Questions

What is Havyu?

Havyu is a two-pillar fitness ecosystem combining frictionless strength training logs with daily habit accountability. Your lifting consistency reinforces your good habits, keeping your training progression moving forward seamlessly.

What is included in the Free tier vs. Pro tier?

The Free tier includes full access to manual logging, habit checking, progress charts, history logs, and the habit scheduler, alongside limited daily access to our AI Routine Builder. Upgrading to the Pro tier fully unlocks the AI Coach Hub, AI Program Review, personalized pre-workout briefings, and advanced weekly nutrition insights.

How is my workout and habit data secured?

Your fitness logs and daily check-ins are stored in a secure local database on your device. If you choose to sign in with an account, your data is securely backed up using cloud infrastructure so you never lose your training history.

How do I delete my account and personal data?

We respect your data privacy completely. You can delete your account permanently at any time by navigating to Settings > Account & Sync within the app and selecting 'Delete Account'. Doing so will immediately and completely wipe your cloud records and clear all local device storage database footprints.
