Delete Your Account & Data

This page explains how to permanently delete your Havyu account and the data associated with it. Havyu is developed by Havyu (com.havyu.app).

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the Havyu app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the Account & Sync section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation prompt, then confirm. Your account and associated data are deleted immediately and permanently.

You must be signed in to delete your account. If you used Havyu without signing in, no account exists and all your data is stored only on your device — uninstalling the app removes it.

What gets deleted

Deleted Your account and sign-in identity (Apple or Google), permanently removed from our authentication system. This is immediate — there is no recovery period.
Deleted All training and habit data stored on your device (workouts, routines, habit history, progress, and settings), erased from the device during the deletion process.
Deleted Your subscription identity link with our payments provider (RevenueCat), disconnecting your account from any entitlement records.

We do not retain a backend copy of your training or habit data. Havyu stores this data locally on your device, not on our servers.

What may be retained, and by whom

Retained Purchase and transaction records held by Apple, Google, or our payments provider for legal, tax, and accounting purposes. These are retained by those providers under their own policies and are not used to identify you within Havyu after deletion.

To cancel an active subscription, manage it directly in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings — deleting your Havyu account does not cancel a subscription billed through the store.
